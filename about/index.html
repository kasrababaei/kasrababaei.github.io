Meet the man behind the code

I’m a part-time comedian to a captivating stubborn red Pembroke Welsh Corgi named Ginger, full-time iOS developer, and master of multitasking between family and Swift syntax.

My journey as an iOS developer began in 2013 when I had to make an app for pedestrians with routing features as part of my Masters project. Learning Objective-C in just three months was tough, but I fell in love with app development right away.

After graduation, I made an expense-tracking app called “My Cost”. I maintained it for a while until I decided to pursue a Ph.D. in Computer Science, focusing on finding anomalies in big data using neural networks.

Throughout my tenure as a freelance iOS developer, I had the privilege of collaborating with various companies, taking projects from conception to completion. Each app listed represents a culmination of my skills, creativity, and dedication to delivering high-quality mobile experiences. While I’ve moved on from contractor work, the responsibility for maintaining these apps now rests with other developers.

In 2020, I made the leap to become a full-time iOS developer and joined Neo Financial, a company dedicated to revolutionizing the banking experience for Canadians. After almost five years, in 2025, I decided to seek a new challenge; therefore, joined the Platform team at theScore.
